Wicky Wows is a “Wiki” type website where people can make their own wikis in order to push out information to consumers of their content. This is done by prompting the developer/s to make said wiki and to give permissions to edit their wiki. A wiki can contain anything as long as it abides by the rules set by the developer/s.
