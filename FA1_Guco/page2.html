Boss 2

Hermit

  • Hermit is the 2nd boss of Shadow Fight 2. He runs a martial arts school in the Secret Path. His students are amazed at what unknown power he wields.
  • Hermit can be a really annoying boss for newer players.
    • His swords are quick and reliable. These can also enflict a status effect that can make your attacks weaker.
    • He uses two types of magic: a lightning arrow and lightning summons. These both do a lot of damage.
  • After defeating Hermit, you move on to the Trail of Blood. While Butcher awaits.
