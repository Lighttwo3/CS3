Boss 1

Lynx

  • Lynx is the first boss of Shadow Fight 2.
  • He runs an assassin organization known as the Order.
  • Lynx serves as a skill check due to his high damage, and enhanced armory
    • His claws can hit you very easily due to the simpler attacks.
    • His claws also have an enchantment which plants a timebomb on you which does VERY HIGH DAMAGE.
  • After defeating Lynx, you can move on to the Secret Path. And with that comes a new threat, known as hermit.
