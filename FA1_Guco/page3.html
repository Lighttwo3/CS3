Boss 3

Butcher

  • Butcher is the third boss of Shadow Fight 2. He runs a gang of young, juvenile people. He misleads them into evil to create his own army.
  • Butcher isn't a very hard boss but there are things you should watch out for about him.
    • His weapons aren't that great at hitting, but they inflict MASSIVE damage and inflict bleeding.
    • His stomps can kill you in one hit but are really avoidable. You can stay close toprevent him from stomping or just jump to not get hit.
  • After defeating him, you move on to a land of pirates, on your way to a new threat, Wasp.
